LPD Item Allocator (Fill Slots, Limit ±25% Variation, Reorder on >±30% Violation)

Allocates items, attempts to fill slots, adjusts allocations to bring items within ±25% variation. If any item *still* exceeds ±30% variation, it re-tries the entire process by reordering items (Amount Asc/Desc, Name Asc/Desc) until a valid allocation is found or all orderings fail.
